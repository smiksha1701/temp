Papers


    Measuring embeddings distortion using KNN graphs

    Johannes Kepler University in Linz


    Numerous methods have been developed to reduce the dimensionality of data for visual exploration. These methods preserve as much information as possible, but they inevitably introduce some distortions. Global indicators have been used to evaluate the overall quality of a map, but they can obscure local distortions. This paper presents an alternative way to quantify and visualize local distortions.


    Analysis of Differences Between Time Tracking APIs

    Johannes Kepler University in Linz


    This thesis compared CPU time and wall time measurements using different APIs. The results showed that all APIs provided highly precise results with negligible variance. The choice of API has minimal impact on the precision and consistency of the measurements.
